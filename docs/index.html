Your data stays yours.

Stepwise reads your activity with read-only access and keeps it on your device. No servers, no analytics, no selling.

●
Read-only access Stepwise can only read your activity, heart rate, and sleep — it can never change or delete anything.
●
Stored on your machine Your refresh token is encrypted with AES-256-GCM, bound to your device. No plaintext, no cloud.
●
No third parties Data flows from Google straight to your device. We don't collect, transmit, or sell any of it.
